# Module 1: Introduction to Signals and Systems

This module introduces the fundamental concepts of signals and systems, establishing the foundation for understanding why transform methods like the Laplace Transform are essential tools in engineering analysis.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:
- Define and classify continuous-time signals and systems
- Understand the role of differential equations in system modeling
- Recognize the limitations of time-domain analysis
- Appreciate the need for transform methods

## Module Overview

The study of signals and systems forms the backbone of many engineering disciplines. Whether you're analyzing electrical circuits, mechanical systems, or control systems, the mathematical framework remains consistent. This module provides the essential background needed to understand why transform methods are indispensable tools for engineers.
