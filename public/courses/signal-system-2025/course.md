# Signal and System 2025

This comprehensive course covers the fundamental concepts of signals and systems, providing students with the mathematical tools and practical knowledge needed for signal processing and system analysis.

## Course Overview

Signals and systems form the foundation of modern communication, control, and signal processing applications. This course provides a thorough understanding of how signals can be represented, manipulated, and analyzed, and how systems process these signals to extract information or produce desired outputs.

The course progresses from basic signal concepts through advanced frequency-domain analysis techniques. Students will learn both continuous-time and discrete-time signal processing, preparing them for practical applications in communications, control systems, and digital signal processing.

## What You Will Learn

By completing this course, you will gain a deep understanding of:

- **Signal Fundamentals**: Classification, representation, and basic operations
- **Sampling Theory**: Nyquist theorem, aliasing, and quantization
- **Signal Operations**: Time shifting, scaling, reversal, amplitude operations
- **Energy and Power Signals**: Calculation and classification
- **LTI Systems**: Linearity, time-invariance, impulse response
- **Convolution**: Integral and summation, properties and applications
- **Fourier Series**: Periodic signal analysis
- **Fourier Transform**: Frequency domain analysis
- **System Analysis**: Frequency response and filtering
- **Applications**: Modulation and filtering
