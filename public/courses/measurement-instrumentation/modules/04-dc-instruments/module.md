# Module 4: DC Measuring Instruments

This module covers the fundamental instruments used for DC electrical measurements. Understanding these instruments provides the foundation for all electrical measurement work.

## Module Overview

DC measuring instruments are the most commonly used tools in electrical engineering. From simple analog meters to sophisticated digital multimeters, these instruments measure voltage, current, and resistance in DC circuits. This module explains their operating principles and practical applications.

## Learning Objectives

After completing this module, you will be able to:

1. Explain the operating principles of PMMC meter movements
2. Design voltmeter and ammeter circuits using series and shunt resistors
3. Understand loading effects and sensitivity specifications
4. Describe ohmmeter principles and digital multimeter operation

## Topics Covered

1. **PMMC Meter Movement** - The D'Arsonval movement fundamentals
2. **DC Voltmeters** - Multiplier resistors and sensitivity
3. **DC Ammeters** - Shunt resistors and range extension
4. **Ohmmeters and Digital Multimeters** - Resistance measurement and DMM operation
